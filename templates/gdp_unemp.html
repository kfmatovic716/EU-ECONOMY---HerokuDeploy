The Beeswarm chart is a one-dimensional chart that displays all information in one single axis, much like a collection of datapoints in a scatter plot. This chart can be very useful to visualize many data points at once and can easily show the outliers of the data. There is a downward movement of the swarm over time in this particular dataset of unemployement rates of EU countries from 2016-2019, indicating a recovering unemployment rate. It shows that Greece and Spain were the outliers, which means that their country had suffered the most in the financial crises.
